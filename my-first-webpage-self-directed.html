The first step therefore is to make the black man come to himself;
    to pump life back into his empty shell;
    to infuse him with pride and dignity,
    to remind him of his complicity in the crime of allowing himself to be
    misused and therefore letting evil reign supreme in the country of his birth.

    This is what we mean by an inward-looking process.
    This is the definition of “Black Consciousness.”

    ~I Write What I Like, Steve Biko
